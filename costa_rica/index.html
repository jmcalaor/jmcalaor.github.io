The effects of fragmentation on bird coloration

Native American and Pacific Islander Research Experience (NAPIRE) Program - Summer 2016

I participated in the NAPIRE Program in 2016. I worked with Dr. Szymek Drobniak to understand the effects of fragmentation on bird coloration. Forest fragmentation has increased with expanding agriculture and can have effects on biodiversity. We were interested in how habitat characteristics of these fragments might influence coloration.
