Research

Current Research

My research evaluates the impacts of bird loss on arthropod food webs, particularly digging into changes in the arthropod community, shifts in predator diets, and the role of spider mesopredators in a landscape without birds. I use field experiments, DNA metabarcoding, and ecological modeling to inform my questions.

  Arthropod Community           Predator Diets             Spider Mesopredators
